    Beatrice Luigi Gomez is the first openly bisexual candidate to win the title of Miss Universe Philippines. Continue reading to explore more about her personal life.

    On Thursday, September 30, Cebu City’s Beatrice Luigi Gomez was titled Miss Universe Philippines.

    The 26-year-old beauty queen’s triumph is a watershed moment for the LGBTQ+ community since she has previously stated in interviews that she is a lesbian.

    Gomez also said in a Cebu Daily News report that as she grew older, she discovered she was attracted to both sexes.

    Beatrice Luigi Gomez Instagram Explored

    Gomez is very active on Instagram and goes by the name @beatriceluigigmz. She currently has 168k followers with 149 posts.

    She also defined herself as non-binary in an Instagram post, writing: ” non-binary… sometimes I’m Beatrice/Bea, other days I feel like being Luigi. It’s about being feminine and masculine at the same time.”

    Beatrice Luigi Gomez Height And Age

    Gomez is a tall woman who stands at 5’9″ and has a balanced physique.

    However, there is no information on the internet about her real weight. Yet, based on her body shape, she must weigh between 50 and 60 kg.

    Beatrice Luigi Gomez Birthday And Hobbies

    Gomez was born on February 23, 1995, in San Fernando, Cebu, Philippines. However, there is no picture available on her Instagram handle celebrating her birthday.

    Gomez, in addition to being a beauty queen, is a community development worker and a naval marine reservist as well.

    Gomez is a self-confessed adrenaline junkie. She was a former volleyball varsity player and also do boxing, scuba diving, and MMA.

    Beatrice Luigi Gomez Partner

    Gomez stated during the beauty contest that she and her partner, Kate Jagdon, had been in a relationship for over seven years.

    Kate, unlike Beatrice, does not model, although she is well-known in her own profession. She was born in the Philippines and, according to sources, she is 26 years old.
