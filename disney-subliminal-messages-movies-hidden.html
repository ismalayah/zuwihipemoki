    6) Real Life Topless Woman – The Rescuers

    This one is nothing short of hilarious. In The Rescuers, there’s a scene where the two mice are flying through the city, and if you know the exact moment and can pause it just right, you will see that there’s a (non-animated) topless woman in one of the windows they soar past. The thing about this scene is, it’s so fast and fleeting that I’m curious how someone even found it in the first place? Actually, I don’t even wanna know.

    This is another one that has since been edited out by Disney (which means they own up to it, which is big on their part), and if you watch it now, there’ll be no nudity. Let it be known, though, there is no nudity in the sequel, either. They just put the sexy right in the title on that one: The Rescuers: Down Under. Yup, that pretty much says it all.
