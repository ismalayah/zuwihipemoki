    What Is Bandmanrill Real Name?

    Bandmanrill real name is Siril Pettus and his rap moniker comes from his first name. The rill in his rap moniker is from Siril.

    The name Siril comes from India, Telegu to be exact, and is a boy’s name which means God’s name, or the name of God. People who carry this name are stated to be stable, calm, and detail-oriented.

    Siril is also similar to Cyril which is a Greek name that means lordly and/or masterful. The two meanings are very similar when set side by side each other.

    As for Pettus, it has 7th-century Anglo-Saxon and Olde English origins and literally means a hole or an empty place. In essence, the rapper’s name means God’s name in an empty place, which is very poetic and fitting for the music he makes.

    Where Is Bandmanrill From?

    Bandmanrill is from Newark, New Jersey.
