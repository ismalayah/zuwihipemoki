    What happens if juice is not refrigerated?

    The refrigerator keeps the microbes (bacteria) away from worsening the color, taste, texture, or even juice nutrients. So when you open the juice bottle or jar and don’t refrigerate juice after opening, it will start fermenting over time and reduce the juice shelf life. It’s due to a bacterial attack on juice.
